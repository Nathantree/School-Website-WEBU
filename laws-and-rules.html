GDPR

GDPR protects the user’s personal information. The company must ask for consent to people if they can store their data. It also must say if they use your data for anything such as selling it etc. If they don’t ensure their customers what they do with their data, they might shutdown or get a fine. You must also assure that the data is accurate, and you can’t process more personal data than is necessary. They also must protect people’s personal data.

Music, images and many other things can be protected by a sort of copyright. Copyright protects the item from being used by creators. Due to this we must get un-copyright material to be able to have images etc on our website otherwise it’ll get removed. To be able to use copyright material you need the creator or companies' consent. However, if you put your own creative twist to the work, it may be usable.

LEK

The law regulates radio, television, telephone and data communication. Cookies is the most relevant regulation for us users which means it stores your data in a text file making it easier for you to surf the web. Cookies are often used to customize your advertisement for webpages. The webpages are forced to ask for cookies when you go onto their webpage otherwise it is against the law. The customers also decide if they want to consent or not.
